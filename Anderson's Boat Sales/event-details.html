Spend a Saturday on the water with us. Test-ride the newest surf and wake boats, talk shop with factory reps, and find out what the right boat really feels like — before you ever sign anything.

About This Event

One Day, Every Boat, On the Water at Anderson's Boat Sales.

There's only so much a spec sheet can tell you. Anderson's Boat Sales Demo Day is your chance to actually feel the difference between models — the push of a surf wave, the snap of a wake, the way each hull carves the Delta — all in one place, in one afternoon.

Our team and visiting factory reps will be on hand all day to answer questions, set up rides, and talk through what fits your crew and your budget. Bring the family, bring your boards, and bring your questions. No pressure, no obligation — just a great day on the water.

What to Expect

On-Water Test Rides

Ride the latest Centurion & Supreme models with a captain at the helm — guided by the team at Anderson's Boat Sales.

Factory Reps On Site

Get answers straight from the people who build the boats — plus local insights from the Anderson's Boat Sales team.

Before You arrive

Registration isn't required, but reserving a ride slot guarantees your time on the water — walk-ins are welcome and ride slots are first-come, first-served.

Absolutely — bring the whole crew. Life jackets are provided for all ages and there's plenty to see from the dock between rides.

Sunscreen, a towel and soft-soled shoes. Bring your own board if you have one — otherwise we'll have boards and vests on hand.
