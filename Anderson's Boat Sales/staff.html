Who works at Anderson's Boat Sales

Anderson's is family owned and operated, with 26 employees across the Waterford and Harrison Township stores, including 11 factory-certified marine technicians.

Sales, finance, service, parts and rigging all sit under the same roof at each location, so the person who sells you the boat is standing next to the person who will service it. The company has been owned by the Anderson and Whitfield family since 1996 and has never been part of a dealer group.
