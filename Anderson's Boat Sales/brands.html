The Brand

Michigan's Home for Godfrey

Godfrey has been building pontoon boats since 1958, when it introduced one of the industry's first all-aluminum hulls out of Elkhart, Indiana. Today the lineup spans everything from entry-level Xperience models to award-winning Sanpan luxury pontoons, all engineered around Godfrey's signature iMPACT tube and rail construction.

Anderson's Boat Sales has carried Godfrey since 1996, making us one of the longest-standing Godfrey relationships in Southeast Michigan. Every Godfrey we sell is prepped, rigged, and backed by our own factory-trained service department — not a third party.
