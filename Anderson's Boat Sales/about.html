About Anderson's Boat Sales

Anderson's Boat Sales is a family-owned Michigan boat dealer founded in 1996, with showrooms in Waterford Township and Harrison Township and 26 employees, including 11 factory-certified marine technicians.

The dealership sells and services Moomba, Supra, Regal, Tahoe, Godfrey and Key West — wake and surf boats, bowriders and yachts, pontoons and tritoons, and center consoles — new and pre-owned. Anderson's serves Oakland, Macomb, Livingston, Washtenaw and Wayne counties, and the water they touch: the Cass Lake chain, Pontiac and Union Lakes, Lake St. Clair, Anchor Bay and the Clinton River.

How we got here

Years, not steps — the numbering here is a real chronology.

1996

Anderson's opens on Highland Road

Jim Anderson sells eleven boats out of a two-bay building in Waterford Township and services them himself.

2004

The service department gets serious

Six bays, three certified technicians, and the first winterization season that ran past a thousand boats.

2011

A second generation takes over

Dana Whitfield, Jim's daughter, buys the business and keeps every employee.

2013

Moomba comes aboard

The first surf boats arrive and change what a Michigan summer looks like for a lot of families.

2017

Harrison Township opens

A second store, on Lake St. Clair, with a rigging dock and on-water demos.

2026

Thirty seasons

Twenty-six employees, two stores, six brands, and a fourth generation of some families buying boats here.
