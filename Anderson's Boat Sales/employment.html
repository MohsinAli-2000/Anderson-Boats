What it is like to work at Anderson's

Anderson's Boat Sales is a family-owned Michigan dealership with 26 employees across Waterford and Harrison Township. We hire technicians, sales consultants, parts specialists and rigging staff.

Benefits include medical, dental and vision after 60 days, a 401(k) with a 4% match, paid factory training and certification, employee pricing, and boat use privileges.

Hiring takes about two weeks: a phone screen, a store visit with a working interview, and an offer. Technicians complete a paid bench evaluation.
