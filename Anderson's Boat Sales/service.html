Our Most-Requested Services

From spring start-up to winter storage, our shop keeps Michigan boats on the water all season long.

Winterization

Basic and premium winterization for outboards, I/O and stern drives — fuel stabilization, engine run-up, fogging, cooling system drain and a courtesy inspection of boat and trailer. Antifreeze protection available.

Boat Detailing

Full-service detailing: complete wash and chamois hand dry, power-buffed wax on all fiberglass, UV vinyl treatment, metal shine, window and canvas cleaning, and scum line treatment. Express detailing also available.

Fiberglass & Gelcoat Repair

Damaged fiberglass can be repaired to look brand new. We staff some of the most professional boat body repair specialists in the area — we'll have you back boating in no time.

Propeller Repair

All props brought back to OEM specs — TIG welded by a certified welder, ground, shaped and balanced. Stainless props high-polished at no extra charge.

Canvas & Upholstery

We repair canvas covers, windows, bimini tops and custom canvases — plus our shop now repairs and creates upholstery interiors. Stuck zippers, rips, cloudy glass and missing snaps all fixed.

Shrink Wrapping & Storage

Shrink wrap is the best protection from snow and ice through long Michigan winters. Indoor rack, indoor floor and outdoor storage available.
