Where to find Anderson's Boat Sales

Anderson's has two Michigan showrooms. Waterford is at 4270 Highland Road, Waterford Township, MI 48328, reachable at 248.666.9922. Harrison Township is at 38000 Jefferson Avenue, Harrison Township, MI 48045, reachable at 586.846.4944.

Both stores are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, and are closed Sunday. Saturdays from November through February are by appointment. Sales, finance, service, parts and rigging are on-site at both locations.
